Invitation to public consultation roadshows

  • Nov 23, 2017

Dear valued ECSA registered persons and all interested stakeholders.

The Engineering Council of South Africa, in line with best practice and in conformance with its founding legislation, its strategic plan and its annual performance plan seeks to consult with stakeholders on governance instruments for the purpose of relevance, transformation, efficiencies, effectiveness and integrity.

In undertaking these measures, the Council does so in pursuit of and in the spirit of collaboration, cooperation and consolidation of expertise from its stakeholder base towards fully executing its legislative mandate of regulating the engineering profession and importantly, in being responsive to the feedback received on a regular basis from its stakeholders on how it could be repositioned to meet current and future expectations and demands of the engineering profession.

It is against this backdrop that administration has taken a decision to review the following regulatory instruments:

  1. Voluntary Association (VA) recognition framework
  2. Commitment and Undertaking guidelines in addition to the establishment of the accords with employer bodies
  3. Continuing Professional Development (CPD) policy

In light of the aforementioned, you are therefore cordially invited to attend the planned roadshows close to your location. Venue details will be updated soonest.
