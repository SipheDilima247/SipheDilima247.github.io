Following the repeal of the Engineering Profession of South Africa Act, 1990 (Act No. 114 of 1990), a new Act called the Engineering Profession Act, 2000 (Act No. 46 of 2000) was promulgated on 26 November 2000. The Act became fully effective on 28 August 2001, which was the day of the first meeting of the new Engineering Council of South Africa.
In terms of Section 18(1), as quoted below, the Act empowers the ECSA to register persons in certain prescribed Categories of Registration. The Categories are: Professionals, Candidates and Specified Categories.

Application Process

SPECIFIED CATEGORIES



Lift Inspector

Step 1

Download Application Form


Step 2

First two pages of the application form F 1.1 and F 1.2 fully completed, signed by yourself and commissioned by the commissioner of oaths.


Step 3

Include certified copies of all your qualifications.


Step 4

Certified Copy of your Lift Inspectors Course certificate if obtained.


Step 5

Certified Copies of Apprentice Learnership certificates if obtained.


Step 6

Nominate Two referees ECSA as either as Registered Lift Inspectors or Professional Engineers or Professional Engineering Technologist or Professional Engineering Technicians or Professional Certificated Engineer

  • Request the nominated referees to fully complete the referee report form F 4.1 and F 4.2 and submit same directly to ECSA.

Step 7

Include the following documents in your application

  • Proof of Voluntary Association Membership or Application for Membership (Letter, certificate, Invoice or Receipt.
  • Ensure that the following documents are completed using the templates indicated are included with the application:
  • Summary of Training and Experience report form F 2.2. From first instance of experience after obtaining the first degree till date of submission in a chronological date order (please see application form information sheet F)
  • Training and Experience Report for each Period using form F 2.1 from first instance of experience after obtaining the first degree till date of submission in the same chronological date order as listed on form F 2.2. Each period on a separate form F 2.1 signed by yourself and the relevant supervisor if registered or not (please see application form information sheet F2)
  • Organograms


ECSA’s is currently receiving professional applications electronically through engineer@ecsa.co.za, the application portal for professional applications is currently under development.



Lifting Machinery Inspector

Step 1

Download Application Form


Step 2
  • First two pages of the application form J 1.1 and J 1.2 fully completed, signed by yourself and commissioned by the commissioner of oaths.

Step 3
  • Include certified copies of all your qualifications.

Step 4

Nominate [LM3] two referees ECSA as either as Registered Lifting Machinery Inspector or Professional Engineers or Professional Engineering Technologist or Professional Engineering Technicians or Professional Certificated Engineer.

  • Request the nominated referees to fully complete the referee report form J 4.1 and J 4.2 and submit same directly to ECSA.

Step 5

Certified Copies of Load test and Inspections certificates signed by yourself and a registered Lifting Machinery Inspector.


Step 6

Fully complete the Development Questionnaire on the prescribed form J 2.3 signed by yourself and the relevant supervisor. Each question must be answered accordingly.

Step 7

Complete [LM4] major Inspection and Load Test Report on the prescribed form J 2.4 for each equipment you wish to be registered in signed by yourself and the relevant supervisor. Each question must be answered.


Step 8

Include a Work Schedule for each equipment you wish to be registered in.


Step 9

Record of Inspections done over a period of at least 2 years on form J 2.6


Step 10

Include the following documents in your application

  • Proof of Voluntary Association Membership or Application for Membership (Letter, certificate, Invoice or Receipt.

  • Training and Experience Report for each Period using form F 2.1 from first instance of experience after obtaining the first degree till date of submission in the same chronological date order as listed on form F 2.2. Each period on a separate form F 2.1 signed by yourself and the relevant supervisor if registered or not(please see application form information sheet F2)
