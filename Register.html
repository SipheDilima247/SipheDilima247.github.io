Following the repeal of the Engineering Profession of South Africa Act, 1990 (Act No. 114 of 1990), a new Act called the Engineering Profession Act, 2000 (Act No. 46 of 2000) was promulgated on 26 November 2000. The Act became fully effective on 28 August 2001, which was the day of the first meeting of the new Engineering Council of South Africa.
In terms of Section 18(1), as quoted below, the Act empowers the ECSA to register persons in certain prescribed Categories of Registration. The Categories are: Professionals, Candidates and Specified Categories.

Candidate



Candidate Engineer

Step 1
Download the application form


Step 2
Complete the first two pages of the application form E1.1 and E 1.2, both pages signed by yourself and the commissioner of oaths (include commissioners stamp).

PROFESSIONAL



Professional Engineer

Step 1

Download the Application form: Registration as a Professional Engineering Technologist


Step 2

First two pages of the application form AN 1.1 and AN1.2 fully completed, signed by yourself and commissioned by the commissioner of oaths


Step 3

Nominate two referees. The referees must be registered with ECSA as a Professional Engineer, Professional Engineering Technologist or Professional Certificated Engineer, with at least one must being registered as a Professional Engineer.

  • Request the nominated referees to complete the referee report forms AN 6.1 and AN 6.2(R-03RR-PE) and submit same directly to ECSA.

Step 4

Include Certified Copies of the original BSc(Eng)/B Eng degree, Considering the Qualification(s) you entered, please verify whether:

  • The qualification is listed on ECSA Document E-20-PE as an accredited programme or appears in list B as a recognised programme and that the year in which you were last registered falls into the period of accreditation; or
  • Your qualification is recognised under the Washington Accord. Please consult the list or database of accredits qualification of the relevant Washington Accord signatory for the country in which you obtained the qualification. If you do not qualify under a or b, please change your application type to Educational Evaluation.

Step 5

Include the following documents in your application:
Proof of Voluntary Association Membership or Application for Membership (Letter, certificate, Invoice or Receipt.


Step 6

Ensure that the following documents are completed using the templates indicated are included with the application:

  • Summary of Training and Experience report AN 2.2(R-O3-TES-PE). From first instance of experience after obtaining the first degree till date of submission in a chronological date order(please see application form information sheet A2)
  • Training and Experience Report for each Period using form AN 2.1(R-03-TER-PE). From first instance of experience after obtaining the first degree till date of submission in the same chronological date order as listed on form AN 2.2. Each period on a separate form AN 2.1 signed by yourself and the relevant supervisor if registered or not (please see application form information sheet A2)
  • Mature Applicants may submit Training and Experience Outlines(TEO). See section 4.3 and Table 3 of R-03-PE.
  • Organograms


(ECSA’s is currently receiving professional applications electronically through engineer@ecsa.co.za, the application portal for professional applications is currently under development.)



Professional Engineering Technologist

Step 1

Download the Application form: Registration as a Professional Engineering Technologist


Step 2

First two pages of the application form B 1.1 and B1.2 fully completed, signed by yourself and commissioned by the commissioner of oaths.


Step 3

Include certified copies of your B Tech Degree and National Diploma certificates


Step 4

Nominate two referees, with the permission of the persons concerned, the referees should have personal knowledge of the applicant's work. Referees must be registered with ECSA as a Professional Engineering Technologist or a Professional Engineer, and in exceptional circumstances a Professional Certificated Engineer or a Professional Engineering Technician, of which one must be a direct supervisor.

  • Request the nominated referees to complete the referee report form B4 and submit same directly to ECSA.

Step 5

Complete [LM1] form B 1.3 listing your Tertiary Qualifications.


Step 6

Include the following documents in your application

  • Proof of Voluntary Association Membership or Application for Membership (Letter, certificate, Invoice or Receipt.

Step 7

Ensure that the following documents are completed using the templates indicated are included with the application:

  • Summary of Training and Experience report form B 2.2. From first instance of experience after obtaining the first degree till date of submission in a chronological date order(please see application form information sheet B)
  • Training and Experience Report for each Period using form B 2.1 from first instance of experience after obtaining the first degree till date of submission in the same chronological date order as listed on form B 2.2. Each period on a separate form B 2.1 signed by yourself and the relevant supervisor if registered or not (please see application form information sheet B2)
  • Organograms
  • Include the fully completed Initial Professional Development Report form IPD(B5)
  • Fully complete the Engineering Report form B2.3
  • Fully complete the Education Development Report form B 18 if you do not meet the benchmark requirements


ECSA’s is currently receiving professional application electronically through engineer@ecsa.co.za, the application portal for professional applications is currently under development.
