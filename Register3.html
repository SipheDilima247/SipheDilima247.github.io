Following the repeal of the Engineering Profession of South Africa Act, 1990 (Act No. 114 of 1990), a new Act called the Engineering Profession Act, 2000 (Act No. 46 of 2000) was promulgated on 26 November 2000. The Act became fully effective on 28 August 2001, which was the day of the first meeting of the new Engineering Council of South Africa.
In terms of Section 18(1), as quoted below, the Act empowers the ECSA to register persons in certain prescribed Categories of Registration. The Categories are: Professionals, Candidates and Specified Categories.

Application Process

SPECIFIED CATEGORIES



Fire Systems Protection Inspector

Step 1

Download Application Form


Step 2

First two pages of the application form N 1.1 and N 1.2 fully completed, signed by yourself and commissioned by the commissioner of oaths.


Step 3

Include certified copies of all your qualifications or courses.


Step 4

Nominate two referees ECSA with at least one referee registered as either a Fire Protection System Inspector or Professional Engineers or Professional Engineering Technologist or Professional Engineering Technicians or Professional Certificated Engineer.

  • Request the nominated referees to fully complete the referee report form N 4.1 and N 4.2 and submit same directly to ECSA.

Step 5

Certified Copies of Load test and Inspections certificates signed by yourself and a registered Lifting Machinery Inspector.


Step 6

Fully complete the Development Questionnaire on the prescribed form N 2.3 signed by yourself and the relevant supervisor. Each question must be answered accordingly.


Step 7

Fully completed Major Inspection and Load Test Report on the prescribed form N 2.4 for each equipment you wish to be registered in signed by yourself and the relevant supervisor. Each question must be answered


Step 8

Include a Work Schedule for each equipment you wish to be registered in.


Step 9

Record of Inspections and Test done on form N 2.6.


Step 10

Initial Professional Development Report form N5


Step 11

Include the following documents in your application

  • Proof of Voluntary Association Membership or Application for Membership (Letter, certificate, Invoice or Receipt.
  • Ensure that the following documents are completed using the templates indicated are included with the application:
  • Summary of Training and Experience report form N 2.2 in a chronological date order (please see application form information sheet H)
  • Training and Experience Report for each period using form N 2.1 in the same chronological date order as listed on form N 2.2. Each period on a separate form N 2.1 signed by yourself and the relevant supervisor if registered or not (please see application form information sheet N2)
  • Organograms
